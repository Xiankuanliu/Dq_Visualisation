Dynamic Visualisation for Clarke and Park Transforms¶

The purpose of this project is to dynamically visualise the Clarke Transform and the Park Transform.

The Clarke Transform was invented by Edith Clarke , who was a remarkable female engineer.

The Park Transform was invented by Robert H. Park .
